Welcome to Our AI in Education Initiative

Target Audience: Welcome all educators, parents, students, and lawmakers affiliated with education! We aim to provide the best information about AI for your curiosity and educational benefits.

Purpose of this Website: Our goal is to educate on the implementation of AI in classrooms, providing current and relevant insights into what AI is, how it impacts education, and presenting arguments for and against its use.

Who Are We? We are a group of Graduate students at IU Indianapolis’ School of Library and Informatic Sciences. Having spent the past semester exploring the benefits of a reliable and accessible website, we collaborated to create this platform to inform about AI in education.

Why This Topic? With AI's growing role in various sectors, understanding its impact on daily life is crucial. We aim to provide insights into the benefits and challenges AI introduces to everyday activities, especially in education.

Why Focus on AI in Education? Education may not have been the first sector impacted by AI, but the rise in its use—and the controversies it brings—demands a thorough exploration of its effects. This site aims to provide answers and insights into AI’s role in the classroom.
