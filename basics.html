What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) encompasses technology designed to mimic human behavior and intelligence. This includes responding to user inputs, solving problems, and making decisions.

Who Created AI?

The concept of AI dates back to ancient times, but modern developments began in the 1920s with the exploration of computational machines. By the 1950s, pioneers like Alan Turing and Arthur Samuel laid foundational work, leading to further advances by John McCarthy, the creation of LISP, and the establishment of the AAAI.

Everyday AI

AI is integrated into various daily technologies, from automatic doors to virtual assistants on our phones, affecting our interactions and the services we use without always being obvious.
