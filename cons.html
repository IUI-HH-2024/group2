Challenges of AI in the Classroom

Cheating: AI tools like ChatGPT and Claude have enabled students to bypass traditional writing processes, leading to increased cheating and challenges in enforcing academic integrity policies.

Lack of Writing Skills: With AI capable of generating complete essays, students are increasingly showing deficiencies in their writing skills and understanding of how to structure various assignments.

Educator Frustration and Jeopardized Relationships: Educators often express frustration with AI-generated assignments, which can lead to distrust and damaged relationships if students pass off AI work as their own, especially where AI use is prohibited.

Conflict with State Standards: Many educational standards, including specific writing benchmarks, may not be met if students rely heavily on AI for assignment completion, posing a conflict with mandated learning goals.
